Datasets

A collection of planning related datasets.

National datasets

These datasets are made of data published by multiple organisations. The data is collected and collated by Digital Land.

  • Area of outstanding natural beauty

    A collection of all the areas of ourstanding natural beauty published by Natural England.

  • Article 4 directions

    The start of a collection of article 4 directions collected from publishing local authorities.

  • Battlefield

    A collection of all the battlefield sites published by Historic England.

  • Brownfield Land

    A set of brownfield land records collected from 350+ local authorities.

    Explore shape of dataset

  • Brownfield sites

    A collection of geographies that are Brownfield land sites. We currently have ~2500.

  • Conservation areas

    A collection of conservation areas collected from local authorities.

  • Developer agreements

    A collection of developer agreement data collect from the local authorities who publish the data.

  • Developer agreement contributions

    A collection of developer agreement contributions. Contribution data is collected from local authorities. All contributions link back to agreements.

  • Developer agreement transactions

    A collection of developer agreement transactions.Transaction data is collected from local authorities. Transactions are associated with a particular contribution.

  • Development plan documents

    A collection of all the development documents local authorities publish. We currently collect these for Buckinghamshire, Lambeth and Southwark.

  • Development policies

    A collection of the policies contained in the development plan documents we know about.

  • Heritage at risk

    A collection of all the heritage at risk sites published by Historic England.

  • Local authority district

    A collection of geogrpahies that represent the area each local authority covers.

  • Local plans Archived

    A collection of data about each local authorities local plans.

    Explore shape of dataset

  • Organisations

    A collection of central and local government organisations who produce planning related data.

  • Park and garden

    A collection of all the parks and gardens published by Historic England.

  • Protected wreck site

    A collection of all the protected wreck sites published by Historic England.

  • Scheduled monunment

    A collectin of all the scheduled monuments published by Historic England.

  • Site of special scientific interest

    A collection of all the sites of special scientific interest published by Natural England.

  • World heritage site

    A collection of all the world heritage sites published by Historic England.

Lists

Registers and lists kept and maintined by the Digital Land team.

  • Contribution funding status

    A list of statuses to mark developer contribution transactions with. E.g. received or spent

  • Contribution purpose

    A list of allowable purposes for developer contributions.

  • Developer agreement type

    A list of the different developer agreement types, e.g. Section 106

  • Development plan types

    A list of the different types of development plans.

  • Development policy categories

    A list of categories used to categorise development policies.

  • Ownership status

    The list of ownership statuses given to brownfield land sites.

  • Permitted development right

    Permitted development rights from the legislation.

  • Planning permission status

    A list of planning permissions statuses given to brownfield land sites.

  • Planning permission type

    A list of permission types given to brownfield land sites.

The list of datasets will be added to as new data is collected. You can read more about the datasets we intend to collect.
